Unigram gives you full control over how you compose, send, and manage messages. This page covers everything from basic text formatting to scheduled messages and polls.

## Sending messages

Type your message in the composer at the bottom of any chat and press **Enter** to send. Press **Shift+Enter** to insert a line break without sending.

### Text formatting

Select any text in the composer and apply formatting with these keyboard shortcuts:

| Format | Shortcut |
|---|---|
| Bold | `Ctrl+B` |
| Italic | `Ctrl+I` |
| Strikethrough | `Ctrl+Shift+X` |
| Monospace | `Ctrl+Shift+M` |
| Spoiler | `Ctrl+Shift+P` |
| Link | `Ctrl+K` |
| Plain text (remove formatting) | `Ctrl+Shift+N` |

You can also right-click selected text in the composer to access a **Formatting** submenu with the same options.

<Tip>
Use `Ctrl+Z` and `Ctrl+Y` to undo and redo formatting changes inside the composer.
</Tip>

## Replying, forwarding, and pinning

### Replying to a message

Swipe a message bubble to the right, or right-click a message and choose **Reply**. The reply header appears above the composer. Press `Escape` to cancel.

- Press `Ctrl+Arrow Up` to reply to the last message in the chat without using the mouse.
- Press `Arrow Up` (when the composer is empty) to **edit** your last sent message instead.

### Quoting part of a message

Select a portion of text inside a message bubble, then right-click and choose **Reply** to quote only the selected excerpt. The quoted text appears in the reply header.

### Forwarding messages

Right-click a message and choose **Forward**, or select multiple messages and click the **Forward** button in the selection toolbar. Unigram opens a chat-picker so you can choose one or more destinations. Only messages that the original chat permits to be forwarded will appear in the selection.

### Pinning messages

Right-click a message and choose **Pin**. If you have the necessary permissions in the chat, the message is pinned and a banner appears at the top. In groups and channels you can pin multiple messages; tap the banner to cycle through them or open the full list.

## Editing sent messages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the message editor">
    Press `Arrow Up` while the composer is empty to load your most recent sent message into edit mode. Alternatively, right-click any of your own messages and choose **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Edit the text, adjust formatting, or replace attached media using the controls that appear above the composer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the edit">
    Press **Enter** or click the checkmark button to save. A small **edited** label appears under the message. Press `Escape` to discard your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can edit messages for up to 48 hours after sending (unlimited for your **Saved Messages** chat). Editing a message does not re-send a notification.
</Note>

## Message search

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Search within a chat" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Press `Ctrl+F` inside any open chat to search through that conversation. Unigram highlights matching messages and lets you step through results with the arrow buttons.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search across all chats" icon="list-magnifying-glass">
    Press `Ctrl+E` from the main chat list to open the global search bar. Results are grouped by recent chats, contacts, messages, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Stickers, GIFs, and emoji

Click the **Emoji** button (smiley face icon) at the right side of the composer to open the media panel. It contains three tabs:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Emoji">
    Browse standard and custom emoji. Recently used emoji appear at the top. Start typing in the search box to filter.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stickers">
    Browse your sticker sets. You can search stickers by keyword. Right-click any sticker to preview its full set or add it to your collection.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GIFs">
    Browse trending and recently used GIFs sourced from the Telegram GIF index. Use the search box to find a specific GIF by topic.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
Type `:` followed by a keyword in the composer (e.g., `:smile`) to trigger inline emoji and sticker suggestions.
</Tip>

## Scheduled messages

You can schedule a message to be sent at a specific time, or when the recipient comes online.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open send options">
    Hold `Shift` and click the **Send** button, or right-click the Send button, to reveal the **Schedule message** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a time">
    In the scheduling popup, choose a date and time. If you are messaging a contact (not a group), you also get the option **Send when online**, which delivers the message the next time they open Telegram.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View or cancel scheduled messages">
    Click the **clock icon** at the top of the chat to open the **Scheduled Messages** view. Right-click any scheduled message to edit or cancel it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Scheduled messages are stored on Telegram's servers, not locally. They will be delivered even if Unigram is not running at the scheduled time.
</Note>

## Voice and video notes

| Action | Shortcut |
|---|---|
| Start recording a voice note | `Ctrl+R` |
| Send the recorded note | `Ctrl+R` (press again) |
| Stop recording without sending | `Ctrl+D` |
| Pause / resume while recording | `Space` or `Ctrl+P` |

When recording starts, a waveform indicator appears in the composer area. You can lock the recording into hands-free mode by clicking the **lock icon** that slides up.

To send a **video note** (round video), click the **camcorder icon** that appears next to the microphone button at the right of the composer.

## Polls and quizzes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Insert a poll">
    Click the **attachment paperclip** icon and choose **Poll** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the poll">
    Enter a question, add between 2 and 10 answer options, and choose your settings:
    - **Anonymous voting** — hides who voted for each option.
    - **Multiple answers** — lets voters pick more than one option.
    - **Quiz mode** — marks one option as correct; voters see immediate feedback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the poll">
    Click **Send**. Results update in real time inside the message bubble.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Quiz mode polls cannot be changed to regular polls after sending, and answers cannot be altered once any vote has been cast.
</Warning>

## Message reactions

Hover over any message and click the **smiley face** reaction button that appears, or right-click a message and choose **React**. Tap an emoji to toggle your reaction on or off. Click the reaction counter under a message to see a list of who reacted.

<Tip>
In channels and certain groups, you may have a limited palette of allowed reactions set by the administrator. Outside those restrictions, you can use any emoji as a reaction.
</Tip>
